Research

I work on securing multimodal large language model (LLM) applications against non-textual promptware attacks. My research explores universal, model-agnostic embedding-based guardrails and robustness benchmarks for multimodal systems, focusing on adversarial variants that manipulate images and other non-textual inputs rather than raw text alone.
